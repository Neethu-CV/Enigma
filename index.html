FAQ

Who is eligible to participate in Enigma?

Enigma is open to all college students. Team sizes can range from 2 to 4 members.

Is this an online or offline event?

Enigma is an offline event, taking place at Jyothy Institute of Technology.

Can we form teams with members from different colleges?

Yes, you can form teams with members from different colleges as long as all members are college students.

What is the registration fee for Enigma?

100 per participant

Will meals and refreshments be provided during the event?

Yes, meals and refreshments will be provided for all participants throughout the duration of the event.

What should we bring to the Enigma?

Please bring your college ID, a laptop, charger, any required hardware for your devices, and any personal items you may need during the 24-hour event.

I'm from a different city. Do you provide accommodation?

No, we do not provide accomodation for participants. All travel expenses as well as accomodation near the venue must be handled by the participant. During the 24 hr period of the event however, we will provide all facilities required for a comfortable event.
